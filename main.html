DISCOVER URBAN CRAFT UPRISING

Seattle's Largest Craft Indie Show

WIDE VARIETY

At UCU, now in its tenth year, fans can choose from a wide variety of hand-crafted goods, including women and men's fashion, jewelry, housewares, and more..

QUALITY & ORIGINALITY

Featuring over 150 vendors excelling in the world of craft, art and design, each show is carefully curated and juried to ensure the best mix of crafts and arts, along with quality and originality.

OUR MISSION

Urban Craft Uprising aims to build a local (and beyond) community of artists, crafters and designers by organizing and providing events where indie crafters and artists can connect with their fans. In addition, Urban Craft Uprising promotes other community building activities, including our crafting shows, showcases, classes, events, sponsorships, online presence and other local activities.
